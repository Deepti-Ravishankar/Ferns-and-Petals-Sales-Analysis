Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

Overview
This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard developed to monitor key business metrics and generate actionable insights. It integrates data on revenue, order volume, customer spending, and delivery timelines into a single, easy-to-use interface, enabling informed and data-driven decision-making.

Key Metrics
* Total Orders: 1,000
* Total Revenue: ₹35,20,984
* Average Order Value: ₹3,520.98
* Average Delivery Time: 5.53 days

Key Insights

The analysis shows that sales are strongly influenced by occasion-based demand, with Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan contributing the highest revenue. In terms of product categories, Colors, Soft Toys, and Sweets perform well, while Mugs and Plants show lower demand.
Seasonal trends indicate revenue peaks in February and August, aligned with major gifting events, while mid-year months experience a decline in sales. From a geographic perspective, cities such as Imphal, Dhanbad, and Kaval demonstrate higher order volumes, highlighting key markets for growth.
At the product level, Magnum Set, Dolores Gift, and Dessert Box are the top contributors, suggesting a customer preference for bundled or premium offerings.

Tools Used
Microsoft Excel

Conclusion
This dashboard helps identify patterns in customer behavior, product performance, and seasonal demand, enabling better strategic planning, operational improvements, and sustained revenue growth.
